🏦 TradingAgents Framework

Multi-Agent LLM Financial Trading System — Complete Architecture Overview

🤔 What Does This System Do?

TradingAgents is an AI-powered trading research assistant. You give it a stock or crypto ticker, and it deploys multiple specialized AI agents that independently research the asset, debate their findings, and produce a structured trading signal — just like a team of analysts at a professional trading firm.

🗺️ Your Analysis Journey:

1. You type a ticker (e.g., BTC-USD or NVDA) into the dashboard
2. Four AI analysts independently research it — technicals, sentiment, news, and fundamentals
3. A Bull researcher argues FOR the trade; a Bear researcher argues AGAINST
4. A Research Manager judges the debate and forms an investment thesis
5. A Trader agent creates a detailed trade plan with entry/exit strategy
6. Three Risk Managers (aggressive, neutral, conservative) debate position sizing
7. A Portfolio Manager makes the final call → you get: BUY / SELL / HOLD with confidence %, stop-loss, and take-profit

⚠️ Shorting Analysis: The current framework does not include an explicit SHORT recommendation. The rating scale goes from BUY → SELL. The Bear Researcher argues against investing (highlighting risks & downsides), but the system is designed around long-only positions. A SELL means "exit/avoid" — not "short the asset." To add true shorting, the Trader and Portfolio Manager prompts would need to be extended with SHORT as an additional action.
